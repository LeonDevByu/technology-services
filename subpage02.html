Information security - Your company's most valuable asset

Creating new software and releasing it to the world is a big and exciting step for any company. It takes a lot to create your software and launch, including the actual building and coding, licensing models, marketing and more. However, any great software must be able to adapt to the times. This means properly monitoring and maintaining all features. Since technology is changing at the speed of light, software must keep up with the changes and demands of the market, updating all the tools involved in its operation.

Is your data secure?

Lately, the topic of IT security or cybersecurity has been on everyone's lips in an alarming way. Organizations are not only exposed to great economic losses but on many occasions, they are also exposed to important losses of data and information, which is the most important asset of a company. By computer security, we mean the process by which companies try to protect the privacy, security, and integrity of the information and data contained in their computer systems, for this reason, it needs professionals capable of preventing the extraction of data illegally.
